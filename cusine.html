Discover the rich flavors and diverse culinary traditions that make Maharashtrian cuisine a delightful journey for your taste buds.

Vada Pav

A true Mumbai street food icon ‚Äî a deep-fried potato patty (vada) is stuffed inside a pav (bread bun) and served with spicy garlic chutney and green chili. It's quick, cheap, and full of flavor.

Puran Poli

A festive flatbread filled with sweet lentil (chana dal) and jaggery mixture, flavored with cardamom. Served with ghee or milk, Puran Poli is a Maharashtrian must-have during Holi and Diwali.

Misal Pav

This spicy and tangy dish consists of a sprouted moth bean curry topped with crunchy farsan, onions, and lemon, served with soft pav. It's a popular breakfast or brunch, especially in Pune and Kolhapur.

Modak

A beloved sweet dumpling made of rice flour and stuffed with coconut and jaggery, Modak is especially prepared during Ganesh Chaturthi as an offering to Lord Ganesha. It represents purity and devotion.
